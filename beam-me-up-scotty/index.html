About

Capt'n Kirk would be a huge Instagram fan. :)

Instructions: Click anywhere on the map to show available images in the radius (green circle)... or ask Scotty to beam you somewhere else by clicking on button :). Also you can see in the realtime where other people are clicking on the map (red circles).

Use button to delete complete "beaming" history and all users will instantly see the change... thanks firebase.io :).

Each time you click on the map button will show you the number of the results returned by the Instagram. Likewise, button will show you the total number of the results for all the users currently using the app.

Cheers!!!
